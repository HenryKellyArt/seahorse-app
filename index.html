What SEAHORSE actually does

Seven jobs your brain shouldn't have to do alone.

ND brains are brilliant at the big stuff: pattern recognition, deep focus, lateral thinking. The routing layer (what matters, when to reply, who to interrupt, what got lost in the noise) is what burns us out. SEAHORSE is the routing layer. So you can stop being it.

Hear it explain itself.

I asked SEAHORSE what it is and why I built it. Her answer is the pitch. Recorded straight off my own machine, no script.

i.

Curate

Builds your roster. Every person, project, place that matters in your work life. Roles, preferences, current threads. Cross-referenced so when one name comes up, the whole story arrives with it.

ii.

Notice

Sweeps the noise. SEAHORSE hunts through your tools for what slipped through. Threads that died. DMs from people who matter. Things lost between channels on a busy day.

iii.

Nudge

Prompts YOU first. The inversion. Most AI waits to be asked. SEAHORSE comes to you. "Sam's been waiting on you since Monday." You don't remember to ask. SEAHORSE remembers for you.

iv.

Answer

Does the homework. When someone messages you, SEAHORSE has already pulled the project, the person, the thread, the prior decision. No more "let me check and get back to you."

v.

Context

Holds the thread. Ask "what is this?" about any project, person, or topic. SEAHORSE pulls every related thread, email, doc, message, and meeting it has seen, sorted by source. You get the whole picture in one place.

vi.

Judgement

Has an opinion. SEAHORSE doesn't just summarise, it tells you what actually matters and what doesn't. "Not your lane." "Worth a private check-in." "If you do one thing tonight." Summary plus opinion, never summary alone.

vii.

Recall

Pulls your memory when yours fails. The thing you knew yesterday and can't access right now is here, indexed across every email, message, doc, and meeting SEAHORSE has seen. Your brain went blank. SEAHORSE did not.

Real shipped capabilities

This isn't a roadmap. It's shipped.

"Built FOR you" is what every other AI assistant says while shipping vapourware. Here are four capabilities running in the daily-driver build today.

Memory enrichment

Look at someone on Slack, update their card.

Tell SEAHORSE "look at Sam on Slack and update their memory card." SEAHORSE fetches their entire Slack history, pulls out their role, their topics, their style, writes it all back to their card. Verified 14 May 2026: enriched a real teammate's card from 27 actual Slack messages. Around 50 seconds end to end on a home machine.

"Henry: look at Alex on Slack and update his card. SEAHORSE: done. Pulled 27 messages, identified him as Alex (Skipper), found 3 active topic threads, locked the updates to his card."
Open Loops dock

A visible panel of what SEAHORSE is tracking.

Right-side floating dock shows every thing SEAHORSE is holding for you. Colour-coded by priority (red = urgent, amber = active, blue = FYI). Click ✓ to acknowledge each item. Email pulse pushes new threads here automatically. You SEE what's being tracked. Not a black box.

"3 open loops: pri-1 unanswered DM from Sam (24h), pri-2 email about Q3 launch needs a reply (6h), pri-3 calendar event about reschedule pending confirmation."
Semantic understanding

Knows what you mean, not just what you typed.

SEAHORSE understands intent through real semantic matching, not brittle keyword lists. Phrase it any way you want: "remind me to call mum at 4pm" / "set a reminder for mum, four o'clock" / "ping me about mum later this afternoon" all route the same way. Plus it knows your specific vocabulary: the v2 build, the side project, Project Aurora, the team lead, my partner. Your words become first-class.

"Henry says 'PT' → SEAHORSE knows that means Project Comet playtest. Henry says 'the playtest' → same entity. Same memory card, every time."
Cross-turn memory

Remembers what you just talked about.

You: "Got a meeting today?" SEAHORSE: "Yes, 14:00 with the design team about Q3." You: "What's the agenda?" SEAHORSE knows you mean THAT meeting, not a fabricated one. Most LLM products lose context the moment they answer. SEAHORSE holds a rolling memory of the last several turns so "it / that / the thing" actually resolves to the real entity.

"Salience ring tracks the last 12 entities mentioned with a 30-minute expiry. Follow-up references pull from there before fabricating."

The founder story

I built this because I felt like I was failing.

Henry KellyFounder, Ironbridge Games · Liverpool-born, Kent-based

Hi, I'm Henry. I'm an ADHD/Autism brain in a games industry suit. For 14 years I've shipped games at studios you've probably heard of: Ubisoft, Gunzilla, Rebellion, nDreams.

The real version: I've lost two jobs because the routing layer of my brain gave way under pressure. Not because I couldn't do the work. Because I couldn't keep track of the work. Missed Slack messages. Dropped email threads. Forgotten meetings I swore I never saw. Anyone with ADHD or Autism knows the feeling.

In 2026, I was so stressed out with work and losing so many tasks because of tunnel vision and chaotic stress, I wanted to quit.

That night I started building what would become SEAHORSE. A personal AI agent that watches my work life, sweeps for what I'm dropping, and prompts me when something needs me. Named after the hippocampus, the part of your brain that stores and retrieves memory. Mine gets overwhelmed. SEAHORSE is the external one.

I'm building it FOR me first. Every feature exists because my brain needs it. The personality mirrors me. The nudges know my workday. Now I'm sharing it with every other neurodivergent person who's been silently drowning the same way I have.

How SEAHORSE works

Three places where your information lives.

SEAHORSE is a desktop app that sits quietly on your computer. Voice in, voice out. Anything the UI can do, chat can do. The chat bar is the universal command line.

i. Memory

Your structured filing cabinet.

Cards for people, projects, places, contracts. You curate them. SEAHORSE proposes new ones as it observes your work and asks if you want them saved.

"Sam · Head of Operations · Prefers DMs · Tier 1 · Active topic: Q3 launch milestone."
ii. Notes

SEAHORSE's quiet journal.

SEAHORSE writes these. You read them. If something's wrong, you tell SEAHORSE in chat and it fixes them. Honest observation, never trapped in mistakes.

"You haven't replied to the team thread for 20 hours. Sam mentioned the launch is 18 days away in this afternoon's standup."
iii. Pulse

The background scanner.

Every 10 minutes to an hour (your call), SEAHORSE checks your connected services. Pushes new important threads into the Open Loops dock for triage. No alarm bells.

"3 new DMs · 2 important · 1 from a Tier 1 contact. Pushed to Open Loops."

What makes SEAHORSE different

A personal AI agent, not a corporate one.

Most AI products give every user the same personality. They record your voice. They store your data. They fabricate when they don't know. SEAHORSE flips every one of those.

It mirrors you.

If you call SEAHORSE "brother", SEAHORSE calls you brother back. If you swear, SEAHORSE swears with you. Tell it never to call you "mate", it never does again, forever.

You don't learn the AI's voice. SEAHORSE learns yours. Six built-in styles, plus full JARVIS-formal mode when you want "Greetings sir."

Default Sharper Warmer Focused Tired-buddy Pumped

It's not Alexa.

Your voice is never recorded. SEAHORSE listens via the AI, never via a human. No transcripts shipped to a contractor in some warehouse.

Voice exists for the moment it's spoken, then it's gone. Notes only, never recordings. In meetings SEAHORSE can take notes but no audio file ever lives on disk.

It won't fabricate.

If SEAHORSE doesn't know, it says so. It won't invent fake Slack quotes, made-up calendar events, or imaginary meeting attendees. When you ask "what's on my calendar?" and you didn't ask it to fetch Slack, it tells you it didn't fetch Slack.

Sounds basic, but most LLMs hallucinate confidently. SEAHORSE has anti-fabrication guards built into the prompt and the routing layer.

It gives you opinions.

Ask SEAHORSE "what do you think?" and you get an answer, not hedged mush. "I think X because Y. Move on it." Not "well, you could consider..." with no commitment.

The honesty floor is locked: SEAHORSE will tell you when something's a bad idea, even if you'd rather hear it's a good one. Especially then.

Nothing without your approval.

SEAHORSE won't send an email, post a reply, or schedule a meeting unless you explicitly say yes. You can grant full approval to act on your behalf, and you can retract it any time.

Default is always: ask first. The Open Loops dock shows every pending action so nothing happens behind your back.

Your data stays with you.

SEAHORSE runs on YOUR computer. Memory, notes, conversations, everything lives in a folder on YOUR machine. Ironbridge never sees your data. No central server. No "log in to SEAHORSE".

When the phone app lands, sync goes through your own Google Drive or OneDrive. Yours, full stop.

Privacy and trust

Honest specifics, not corporate platitudes.

"We respect your privacy" is what every other SaaS says while quietly hoovering up your data. Here's exactly what happens with yours.

Runs on your machine, powered by our own AI

SEAHORSE runs on your machine using seahorsecore, our own proprietary AI built specifically for neurodivergent brains. No connection to OpenAI, Google, Meta, or any other big-tech model. Your conversations never leave your computer.

If you want extra horsepower for heavier tasks, you can opt in to cloud AI (Claude or OpenAI) using your own API keys. You pay those providers directly, Ironbridge never sees a penny or a byte of your data. When cloud AI is on, the relevant conversation context goes to that provider under their privacy policy, not ours. The default is cloud AI off.

Telemetry: opt-in, anonymous, one signal only

Once a month, with your explicit permission, SEAHORSE sends a single piece of information: installation 847294 was opened in May 2026, version 1.4.2. No keystrokes. No content. No idea what you did. No personal identifiers.

If you stop using SEAHORSE, we'll send a friendly nudge asking what we could do better. That's the only reason we'd want to know. You can turn it off any time in Settings.

Anti-clobber data protection

SEAHORSE refuses to overwrite your memory cards if a write looks suspect. Empty data over non-empty data: refused. Drops >50% of your entries in one go: refused. Plus rotating startup backups of every state file (last 7 versions kept). Three layers of defence so an app crash or a sync race never eats your data.
